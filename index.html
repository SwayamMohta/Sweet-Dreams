Sweet Dreams

Dreams as "sweet" experiences connect directly to the theme of transformation, particularly the way fragmented, fleeting dream memories are reshaped into coherent narratives. The sweetness of dreams refers not only to their often pleasant or surreal nature but also to their delicate, ephemeral quality—how they linger just long enough to stir emotion before dissolving upon waking. This sweetness captures both the allure and the transience of dreams, highlighting their power to briefly transport us to alternate realities. In representing this transformation—from scattered mental images into full stories—the theme underscores our innate desire to make sense of the abstract and hold on to what is otherwise intangible.
